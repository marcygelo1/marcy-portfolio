Academic Biography

I am Marcy Angelo M. Villegas, a graduate of Espiritu Santo Parochial School (ESPS). I am currently pursuing my studies at the Technological Institute of the Philippines (TIP), taking up the Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (BSCpE) program. I chose this field because I discovered a strong passion for technology, problem-solving, and the continuous development of innovative digital solutions.

Throughout my academic journey, I have built a solid foundation in both technical and analytical skills. I have successfully earned certifications in Computer Networks 1, 2, and 3, as well as Cybersecurity 1, which strengthened my understanding of network systems, security principles, and modern computing environments. Additionally, I have completed training in Basic Occupational Safety and Health (BOSH), equipping me with knowledge of workplace safety standards and responsible professional practices.

As a Computer Engineering student, I am continuously improving my skills in web development, particularly in frontend technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I am passionate about creating user-friendly, responsive, and visually appealing interfaces that enhance user experience.

I am a dedicated and motivated learner who values growth, adaptability, and continuous improvement. My goal is to further develop my expertise in both software and hardware systems, while contributing to innovative projects that make a meaningful impact in the field of technology.
