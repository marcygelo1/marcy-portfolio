I am Marcy Angelo M. Villegas, a graduate of Espiritu Santo Parochial School (ESPS). I am currently pursuing my studies at the Technological Institute of the Philippines (TIP), taking up the Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (BSCpE) program.

Throughout my academic journey, I have built a solid foundation in both technical and analytical skills. I have successfully earned certifications in Computer Networks 1, 2, and 3, as well as Cybersecurity 1, which strengthened my understanding of network systems, security principles, and modern computing environments.

As a Computer Engineering student, I am continuously improving my skills in web development, particularly in frontend technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I am passionate about creating user-friendly, responsive, and visually appealing interfaces that enhance user experience.
